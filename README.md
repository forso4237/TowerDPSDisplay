### Shows a tower's live DPS and average DPS (and optional cash/second) right next to its Damage and Cash counters in the tower selection menu.

* Live DPS shown next to the vanilla Damage ("pops") counter
* Average DPS measured across the tower's lifetime
* Cash / second readout for income towers like Banks and Farms
* Everything is toggleable in the in-game Mod Settings
